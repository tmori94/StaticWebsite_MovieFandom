Welcome to the fandom page of Blue Steel, a black and white Western movie directed by Robert N. Bradbury, 1934.

This page aims to gather together the community of fans of this movie around the world. Enjoy the descriptions, watch some extract of the movie itself.

John Wayne plays a U.S. Marshal trying to capture the Polka Dot Bandit, who has taken off with $4,000. The movie is also known as An Innocent Man or Stolen Goods in the USA.
